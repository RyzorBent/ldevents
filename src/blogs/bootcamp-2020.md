We had our very first coding bootcamp in 2020, and what a joy it was to see everyone listening intently to the presenter. The audience was a mixture of young and old from 9years - 40 years. And questions where coming form the whole spectrum of age groups. Learners were edified by the speakers worlds that "Everyone is a genius, everyone can rise to the highest level of competency"

### Topics Covered

Mr Swanepoel touched on the topic of basic maths that is needed just for understanding of how machines makes computations inside using a system knon as "binary", were a computer checks the amount of current in the board's wires to decide whether to alocate a zero or one, Which is the basis of most computers.

Leaners were also encouraged that one does not need to have a university degree to get employment as a computer programmer or robotics engineer. A college certificate and a lot of determination is good enough.
